Scepter

a direct democratic system

01 — Truth

Look at the thing itself and call it what it is. He who waits to be told what is true has given himself away.

02 — Sovereignty

Reason dealt no man a greater share of the law than his neighbor. What is common to all should be decided by all.

03 — Onus

You chose; therefore bear it. The harvest of your own judgment is simply yours. To carry your share of the common weight without complaint is what man is for.
